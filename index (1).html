Frequently Asked Questions

Our 4-step verification includes identity confirmation, background check, skills assessment, and reference checks. This rigorous process typically takes 3-5 business days and helps ensure pet owners can trust our caregivers.

You can specify any special needs in your pet's profile and when requesting services. Our matching system will prioritize caregivers with relevant experience, whether it's medication administration, senior pet care, or behavioral issues.

Pet owners pay through our secure platform. Caregivers receive payments weekly via direct deposit. We hold payments until the service is completed to ensure satisfaction. Our service fee is 10% for caregivers (you keep 90%).

All caregivers undergo background checks. Services include GPS-tracked walks, photo updates, and secure messaging. We also offer premium insurance options and 24/7 support for emergencies.
